About

Liminality is a first-person psychological horror game inspired by the Backrooms. Set in a procedurally generated, backrooms-like maze, navigate the eerie hallways haunted by flickering lights and unsettling sounds. Restore power by activating five power breakers, unlocking the passageway to escape this reality. But beware — statue-like entities stalk you silently, moving only when unseen, seeking to keep you suspended in the in-between for eternity.

Controls

Use WASD to move, Mouse to look.
Press Escape to pause, Space to jump.
Press E to interact with highlighted objects.
Press F to toggle flashlight.
Press ` (tilda) to open developer console.
